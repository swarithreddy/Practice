SafeRide – Women’s Safety Smart Band
Slide 1 – Title

Title: SafeRide – Women’s Safety Smart Band
Subtitle: Empowering women’s safety through smart wearable technology
Visuals: A smart band, women traveling safely, tech & safety theme

Slide 2 – Problem

Heading: The Problem
Points:

Women’s safety during travel, especially at night, is a major concern in India.

In emergencies, reaching for a phone isn’t always possible.

Need for a quick-response wearable that instantly alerts family and authorities.
Visuals: Concerned woman, unsafe travel scenario, emergency icon

Slide 3 – Solution

Heading: The SafeRide Solution
Points:

Smart wearable band ensuring women’s safety.

SOS button sends real-time alerts to registered contacts and police.

Tracks GPS location and monitors heartbeat for stress detection.

Mobile app for live tracking and emergency response.
Visuals: Smart band + phone app interface + GPS tracking visuals

Slide 4 – Key Impact

Heading: Impact and Benefits
Points:

Enables rapid emergency response.

Builds confidence for solo female travelers.

Reduces response time during distress.

Promotes smart tech adoption for public safety.
Visuals: Women feeling safe, emergency response icons

Slide 5 – Technology Used

Heading: Core Technology Stack
Points:

Arduino / ESP32 microcontroller

GPS & GSM modules for alerts and location

Heartbeat sensor for panic detection

Rechargeable battery (24-hour backup)

Android app for real-time monitoring
Visuals: Circuit board, sensors, app dashboard
